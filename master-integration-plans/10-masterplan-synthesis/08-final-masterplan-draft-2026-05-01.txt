## Risks / Mitigations
- SSE drift: atomic stream merge and tests.
- Finalize/version races: one client per chat.
- Duplicate viewers/heartbeat TTL skew: one viewerId per tab.
- postMessage timing regression: port local runtime/hooks before aesthetic tweaks.
- product-postcheck duplicate truth: one consumer.
- Env/schema drift: atomic env slice.
- CSP/proxy duplication: single CSP source.
- Design regression: token checklist and grep.
- Test gaps: add P0 route tests when hotspots are touched.

## Final Recommendations
Treat integration as two phases: first clean WIP + merge, then port-ours reapplication and UI-safe smoke. Keep commits small by domain, refresh refs before merge, and write one internal note for intentional hidden/ported master features so future LLMs do not undo them.
