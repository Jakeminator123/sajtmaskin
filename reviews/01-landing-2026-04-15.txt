──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TOP IMPROVEMENTS (concrete, with file:line)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Wire or remove entry-mode selection — `use-landing-controller.ts:112-120` defines `pickCategory`; `use-landing-controller.ts:233` exports it; `chat-area.tsx:25-41` never destructures it — add a minimal segmented control or links in `landing-hero.tsx` (or pass props from `page.tsx`) so mallar/audit/fritext are discoverable without deep links.

2. Align in-page navigation with tab content — `chat-area.tsx:71-88` sticky tab strip has no `id="funktioner"` / `id="teknik"` (grep shows no `id=` in file); fix hashes expected by `SiteNavMenu` (`#funktioner`, `#teknik`) or change menu hrefs to match the Tabs implementation.

3. Tighten motion to the 150–200ms band where appropriate — `landing-feature-blocks.tsx:36` uses `duration-700` for card entrance/hover affordances; consider shorter transitions for hover/focus to match the stated micro-interaction budget (keep longer duration only for scroll-reveal if needed).

4. Reduce hardcoded color literals in `landing-v2` — `particle-orb.tsx:55-124`, `how-it-works-scene.tsx:31-234`, `landing-tech-integration-cards.tsx:44-49`: migrate glows and gradients to `hsl(var(--primary) / …)` / `--brand-*` or document exceptions for WebGL/Three.js.

5. Below-fold density — `chat-area.tsx:155-262` pricing + SajtStudio block + grids: consider progressive disclosure (e.g. single “Mer” expansion) to match Apple-like restraint on first visit.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT WORKS WELL
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• `landing-hero.tsx:77-87` — Very short Swedish copy, strong hierarchy (`font-(--font-heading)`, `text-balance`).
• `landing-hero.tsx:113-189` — Input card `rounded-3xl`, `border-border/50`, `duration-200` on focus; voice/audit branches stay symmetrical.
• `src/styles/landing-v2.css:1-24` — `.landing-cta-primary` centralizes orange glow using `hsl(var(--primary) / …)` and 200ms easing.
• `landing-background.tsx:34-48` — Layered, non-interactive backdrop with semantic tinting via `data-landing-bg`.
• `navbar.tsx:26-31` — Lightweight chrome: blur + border only after scroll, `duration-200`.
• `page.tsx:355-368` — Clean composition: full-height column, `Navbar` + `ChatArea` only (modals aside).
