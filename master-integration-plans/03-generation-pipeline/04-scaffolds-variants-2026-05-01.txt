Date: 2026-05-01

## Master Pipeline Changes
- Scaffold registry contains manifests with files, tags, prompt hints, and allowed build intents.
- Matcher combines keyword and embedding paths with app/auth/ecommerce guards.
- Precomputed scaffold embeddings and parity tests must stay in sync.
- Keyword banks expanded across landing, SaaS, portfolio, blog, dashboard, app, auth, ecommerce, hospitality.
- Scaffold variants load from `config/scaffold-variants/<scaffoldId>`.
- Variant embeddings plus keyword fallback choose style variants.
- Follow-up locks persisted/default variants unless clear-redesign releases.

## Local Behaviors To Preserve
- Follow-up variant stability and default fallback.
- Keyword vs embedding override thresholds.
- No default boost in embedding variant picker.
- Brief nomination vs final variant drift logging.

## Merge/Port Plan
- Keep matcher, search, embeddings, and registry in sync.
- For new scaffold IDs, update registry, manifest, embeddings, keywords, variants, and variant embeddings.
- Validate schema after variant/manifest changes.

## Frontend Wiring / UI Surface Needed

- Follow-up: lock variant unless clear-redesign.
