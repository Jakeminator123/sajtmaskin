Single-Gate Proposal
Single rule: after one canonical deterministic pass and one diagnostic collection, call LLM repair at most once per unique {versionId/runId, contentHash, diagnosticFingerprint} only when a blocking, actionable, file-scoped diagnostic remains and deterministic repair did not clear it.

Verifier-only quality findings should not trigger their own LLM pass unless mapped to an allowlisted blocking class. Server-verify, build-error, partial-file, home-route, tsc/eslint, and syntax should all enqueue diagnostics into the same gate/ledger instead of calling separate fixers.
